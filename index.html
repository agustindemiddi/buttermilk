What is Buttermilk?

Buttermilk is the go-to brand for young people who want to express their love for pop culture in a unique and stylish way. Our t-shirts feature cool designs inspired by popular movies and characters, but with a twist that sets us apart from the rest. We don't do cliché or typical images – instead, we focus on the more insightful moments that true fans will appreciate. Whether you're a hardcore fan or just someone with a young spirit, Buttermilk has something for you. So why settle for boring, generic clothing when you can wear something that truly reflects your personality and interests? Try Buttermilk today and see the difference for yourself.

Now, for real, Buttermilk is just a fictional brand. But it sounds cool, huh? We better clarify quickly that we do not make any money out of this. We don't want to go to jail, or pay fines, nor do we want to have any problem with the law due to copyright problems.

We cannot guarantee, however, that we have not used any pre-written code. But hey, let's be honest, we simply do not have the time to learn the intricacies of binary language, or to comprehend the workings of transistors and their impact on electron flow. Luckily, previous programmers have already laid the groundwork and written code that we can build upon, allowing us to work on a higher level of abstraction.
